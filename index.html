About Us

Audiotonic are a 4 piece Rock & Pop party, festival, function, wedding covers band based in Reading Berkshire UK. We play music from across the years, faithfully performed to an high level of musicianship. We work hard to create the best show for your event and party and love seeing happy people dancing and singing along.
